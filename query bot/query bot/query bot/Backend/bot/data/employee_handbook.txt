Document Title: Employee Handbook – Code of Conduct & Workplace Guidelines
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Office Timings

Regular office hours: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.

Lunch break: 1:00 PM – 2:00 PM.

2. Dress Code

Formal attire from Monday to Thursday.

Smart casuals allowed on Friday.

No shorts, flip-flops, or overly revealing clothing in the workplace.

3. Code of Conduct

Treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect.

No harassment, discrimination, or offensive language tolerated.

Company assets must be used only for official purposes.

4. Probation & Confirmation

All new hires undergo a 6-month probation period.

Confirmation is based on performance review by the reporting manager.
